  • We have built blockchain platform for autonomous cars called Ridechain.

  • Why do we need blockchain here?

  • 1. Access control. You don’t need to rely on third party car sharing app anymore! With blockchain smart contract can easily grant or revoke access to car for person you trust.

  • 2. Historical data. 2nd smart contract stores all data about issues and accidents. Blockchain by its nature proves that this data is not corrupted.

  • Next time you decide to buy such second hand car will be sure you know everything about it.

  • 3. The last smart contract is about Payments. Based on GPS data sent to blockchain, you car will pay your bill wherever you pass a toll or stay on parking lot.

  • We believe autonomous cars with blockchain together will bulid incredible future for us!

  • Drive safely, Help friends, Pay securely with RideChain!
