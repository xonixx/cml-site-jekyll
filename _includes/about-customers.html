Klaus Himmer
CEO AND CO-FOUNDER

At CryptoTax we’re developing a complex taxation product for crypto space in European and International markets. Before approaching CML Team we were working with another agency, and we were not very happy with agility and services provided by that agency and at that moment we’re really concerned if we can build a product before deadline, when German customers report their taxes. One of our co-founders met CML Team at London blockchain hackathon where they were amongst runner-ups. We were impressed by their execution during that hackathon and decided to try working with them.

The task was quite challenging, to dive deeply and quickly to the codebase left from another agency, rewrite some parts of it in a proper way, and finish a number of missing features and core functionality left for MVP.

In the matter of few weeks CML Team were able to understand all the complex business logic behind our CryptoTax product, rewrite crucial backend parts in a proper solid way and in the next few months they were able to implement missing functionality and polish this product to production ready shape.

As a result, we’ve released our product just in time and in a good quality, that brings us good traction and we decided to stick with CML Team as our key development partner for a long- term relationship.

We work with them more than one year and quite happy with agility, quality of the services and insights they provide to us. Now when we’re entering new markets, we know for sure that we can rely on our development partners at CML Team.

What do you like most about the company?

Deep mathematical & computer science skills combined with the ability to dynamically scale in terms of time and professional requirements in a fast-changing startup environment.
