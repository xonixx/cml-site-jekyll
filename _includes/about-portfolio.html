  • Cryptotax

    Solution:

    CML Team implemented core taxation engine for German and Switzerland markets. Backend consists of different complex workflows of tax report fees calculation. A number of operations supported (trades, deposits, withdrawals) as well as a coverage of the most frequent crypto assets use cases (stacking, lending, ICO, hard-fork). The project includes deep integration with popular crypto exchanges, wallets and aggregators including Binance, Bitfinex, Coinbase, Poloniex, Kraken, Ethereum Wallet and many others. Frontend was built using React and Redux with focus on great UX and responsive design.

    Client:

    Cryptotax are enthusiastic about decentralization and the new opportunities enabled by blockchain technology. They want to contribute to this development by offering premium tax reporting solutions for private and institutional investors.

    Description:

    A complex cloud-based taxation product for crypto space in European and International markets.

    Role:

    Full cycle development from requirements specification and architecture to deployment, 3rd line support and maintenance.

    Team and duration:

    Tech Lead and 3 FTE in development team working for 13 months (ongoing).

    Industries:

    Taxation, Crypto.

    Country:

    Germany

    Technologies:

    Java, ReactJS, Spring Boot, Spring Framework, Typescript, Hibernate, ELK, Newrelic, Tomcat, AWS, Postgresql.
